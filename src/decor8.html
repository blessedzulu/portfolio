Decor8

Creating a memorable online shopping experience for an interior design store.

Project Overview

Decor8 is an interior design store and decor company based in Chingola, Zambia, on a mission to make stylishness the norm by making luxury accessible. Despite this bold ambition, they did not have a website of their own. To make their services available to a wider audience and to grow beyond Chingola, they needed a website to reach people in a more convenient way.

I worked with Decor8 to create a website which makes their product catalogue easy to look through using an intuitive user flow and navigation, offers their customers purchasing convenience by allowing them to buy their products from the comfort of their own homes, and puts their decor services on display by showcasing some of their best work.

Visual Design

Luxury has a look and feel, and I had to make the relevant design choices and in order to reflect it. Because Decor8 didn't have a website on which to base the design, everything had to be done from scratch. The typography and the color scheme were of utmost importance, because they set the mood and immediate impression of every design.

Big and sharp serif typography for the most stand-out text to lend a bold feeling of luxury.

Eye-catching colour palette that communicates elegance while staying out of the way of the experience.

Subtle animations that guide and entertain the user as they go about using the website.
